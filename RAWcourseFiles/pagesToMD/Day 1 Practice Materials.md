**Confidence Level Guide:**

- **6.0–7.0: High Confidence** — You have received strong signals of value. Dedicate significant time, resources, and energy to build it. High confidence — I'll continue on this idea.

- **5.0–5.9: Medium** — Some interest and responses are mixed, with open questions still remaining. Run a structured experiment over 90 days. Set clear metrics and effort and eliminate what is lukewarm.

- **Below 5.0: Low** — The response is lukewarm or uncertain and may not be worth pursuing as-is. Process your log and summarize findings. Even a "no" doesn't guarantee success won't come; it tells you where to focus.
