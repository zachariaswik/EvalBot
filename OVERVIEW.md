# EvalBot — Overview

**EvalBot is an AI-powered tool that automatically evaluates startup investment submissions.** Instead of analysts manually reading through pitch decks and documents, EvalBot reads them and produces a structured, consistent assessment in minutes.

---

## How It Works

You drop a startup's submission documents (PDFs, Word docs, plain text) into a folder. EvalBot reads them and runs them through a pipeline of 7 specialized AI analysts — each focused on a different angle of the business. The results are saved to a dashboard and exportable as reports.

---

## What It Analyses

Each startup gets assessed across all of these dimensions:

- **The business case** — What problem is being solved, what's the solution, and how is it positioned in the market?
- **Scores across 10 dimensions** (1–10 each) — things like market size, team strength, product differentiation, traction, and financials — plus an overall verdict
- **SWOT analysis** — Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats
- **Market sizing** — TAM/SAM/SOM estimates and a competitive landscape overview
- **Product assessment** — Is this a real product or just an AI wrapper? How defensible is the positioning?
- **Team & founder fit** — Does the team have the right background and execution ability?
- **Strategic recommendations** — Suggested pivots, and concrete 30-day and 90-day action plans

---

## Batch Mode — Evaluate Many at Once

EvalBot can process an entire batch of startups in one go and produce a **comparative ranking** across the batch. This is useful at the end of a cohort review or deal flow sprint — you get a ranked list with reasoning, not just individual scores.

---

## The Web Dashboard

There's a browser-based interface where you can:
- Upload a batch of startup folders
- Monitor evaluation progress in real time
- Browse results by batch or individual startup
- View scores, verdicts, SWOT analyses, and recommendations
- See a hall of fame of top-scoring startups across all batches

---

## Key Properties

- **Consistent** — Every startup is evaluated against the same framework, removing reviewer fatigue and bias
- **Fast** — A full batch of startups can be processed overnight rather than over weeks
- **Traceable** — Every output is saved with the model used, token costs, and retry history, so nothing is a black box
- **Self-correcting** — If one analyst detects that earlier data was too thin to work with, the pipeline reruns from the right point automatically
